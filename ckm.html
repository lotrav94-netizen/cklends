1% and 0% Down Payment Assistance

CK Mortgage now offers exclusive programs designed to help buyers get into homes with minimal upfront cost. Depending on eligibility, you can qualify for either 1% or even 0% down payment assistance, helping you keep more in your pocket while building equity faster.

1% Down Program

Ideal for moderate-income borrowers. You put down 1%, and qualified assistance programs help with the remainder to reach your full down payment needs.

0% Down Program

Perfect for first-time buyers or qualified essential workers. Get 100% financing with no upfront down payment required, subject to program qualification.

Wholesale vs Retail Savings

Working with a brokerage like CK Mortgage means access to the wholesale side of the lending industry. Instead of being locked into a single bank’s retail pricing, we compare multiple lenders to find you the best possible rate and terms.

Wholesale Advantage

Multiple lender options, more control, and consistently lower rates. Save on fees and maximize your buying power through our network relationships.

Retail Disadvantage

Retail lenders offer only their own programs — often meaning higher interest rates and limited flexibility. With us, the market competes for you.
