DSCR Loans

For real estate investors who qualify based on property rental income instead of personal income. Ideal for scaling portfolios fast.

  • No income verification
  • 20% minimum down
  • Qualification by cash flow ratio

Bank Statement Loans

Perfect for self-employed borrowers. Lenders use bank deposits from the last 12–24 months to calculate qualifying income.

  • 12–24 months bank records
  • As low as 10% down
  • Ideal for entrepreneurs

Conventional 1% Down

Exclusive Rocket Mortgage ONE+ offers a 2% grant on top of your 1% down payment, giving you 3% equity from day one.

  • Only 1% downpayment from you!
  • Rocket contributes 2%
  • Low PMIs compared to FHA

FHA Loans

Backed by the Federal Housing Administration, these help buyers with smaller savings or less-than-perfect credit achieve ownership.

  • 3.5% down with 580+ credit
  • Government insured
  • Low closing costs

VA Loans

For Veterans and service members with zero down, no mortgage insurance, and flexible qualification standards.

  • $0 down payment
  • No PMI required
  • Backed by VA benefits

Adjustable Rate Mortgages (ARM)

Start with a lower interest rate that adjusts after a fixed term. Great choice for those expecting income growth or early payoff.

  • Lower initial rate
  • 5/1, 7/1, 10/1 terms available
  • Fixed then adjusts yearly

Renovation (Reno) Loans

Finance home purchase and improvements in one loan. Ideal for fixer-uppers and upgrading current homes.

  • Combine purchase + renovation
  • FHA 203(k) and CHOICERenovation options
  • Up to 75% of improved value
